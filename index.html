07 / THE HERITAGE

THE REFLECTION BEHIND EVERY EVENT.

Translating Urban Energy Since 2009.

Established in 2009, TNEVE was born out of a desire to translate Mumbai's electric, gritty urban energy into a digital-first discovery ecosystem. We realized early on that people don't want static corporate schedules or boring bus tours—they crave intense, tactile moments of physical connection within the city they live in.

Our philosophy is rooted in "Situational Experiences" that treat streets, beaches, alleys, and neighborhood landmarks as active participants in a narrative. Whether through hidden clue codes, glitched navigation maps, or interactive group trivia, we show you the hidden reflections of the city you think you know.
